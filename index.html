What Is Boltfare

Boltfare is built for the purpose of influencers marketing. Donations and payments from fans and brands get directly deposited to the influencers wallet address for BTFE.Fans and Brands who are interested in working or donating to an influencer can utilize BoltFare to ensure fast and secure payments to the Influencer of their choice.

Play To Earn

Are you a gamer? If so, join BoltFare to make money while gaming. Each user can earn BTFE Tokens by completing a set of challenges and uploading the screen record of it to us. Within 24-48 Hours the promised reward will be added to your BTFE Wallet. In order to Open a wallet please follow this tutorial. Fans, Subscribers and Brands can pay/donate to their favourite Influencers and streamers using BoltFare Token for Completing Challenges. Bolt Fare is based on BSC's blockchain which ensures the security and integrity of the Blockchain and your Coins.
